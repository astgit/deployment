The Flex Mortgage Range

The Flex Mortgage Range is fixed rate with interest rolled up to redemption. Interest is calculated daily and compounded monthly. This delivers significant cashflow flexibility as no monthly payments are required on any of our Flex products.

Our Flex products are available to experienced buy to let, self employed and high net worth* clients.

*Subject to compliance with regulatory requirements. Please note that an APRC is generated as part of the application process.

No btl stress test

Because there are no monthly payments we do not need to apply an additional stress test to buy to let applications.

Income requirement

Proof of income will be requested on application but there is no specific income requirement.

Applicant age

Minimum age on application is 21. There is no maximum age limit.

Flex 3

Flex 3 offers shorter term (up to 2 years) finance as an alternative to conventional bridging. A 5% ERC applies during the first three months only.

All interest is rolled up and paid at redemption.

Product detailsHow it worksCase example
Key details
  • Fixed rates from 8.89%
  • ERC period of 3 months
  • Loan term: 1 or 2 years
  • 1st charge - borrow up to 65% of the property value
  • 2nd charge (combined including any 1st charge mortgage) – varies up to 82.5% LTV (including any rolled up interest) determined on a case by case basis
  • Arrangement Fee of 2%

Flex to Buy

Flex to Buy is designed for property investors who want to be ready to buy new stock quickly when the opportunity arises – at an auction for example. It gives active investors an ability to sign contracts at the drop of a hat, secure in the knowledge that they have a pre-agreed facility in place.

Product detailsHow it worksCase example
Key details
  • Typical rate of 8.99% pa fixed on drawn balances & rolled up
  • First and second charge
  • Borrow up to 70% of existing property value (including rolled up interest)
  • Cost of undrawn facility is limited to a single fee of 2% to secure the funds
  • Minimum facility £250,000
  • Maximum up to £5m

Flex Refurb to Let

Flex Refurb to Let allows landlords to raise capital on property in need of improvement or refurbishment. Provided no planning consent is required, the work proposed can include roofing, rewiring, plastering, new bathrooms & kitchen etc.
